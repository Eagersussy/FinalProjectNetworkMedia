Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced potatoes and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped onions and cook until softened.
  4. In a bowl, beat eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes and onions.
  6. Cook until the edges set, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve.

End Result:

A delicious Spanish omelette ready to be enjoyed!
